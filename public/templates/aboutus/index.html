About Us


"Ideas come and go, stories stay."

We at CultStage are here to enable the creatives write their own story. In the light of this endeavor, CultStage aims to create an organized platform for the employment sector in the Entertainment world such that it provides the networking opportunities a collaborative environment conducive to the success of every film, television and theater creative regardless of geography or experience in India.

The Platform will not just be only about networking, it’s also very much about education. We intend to improve the accessibility, both online and offline, to professional courses available for enhancing creatives’ skill and talent under the right guidance of qualified and motivated instructors and influential people in our community. Cultstage.com is all about empowering the creatives.

Cultstage.com provides each creative an opportunity to connect, collaborate and bridge the gap between amateurs and professional alike. We are creating job opportunities for hobbyists, amateurs and professionals in a scarcely explored and highly unorganized market that has numerous entry barriers.

CultStage, for the first time, gives the Art, Media & Entertainment Industry in India an online platform for Talent acquisition and save millions in the archaic human infrastructure. Our vision is to provide the most exclusive user experience for Professionals and amateurs to connect, collaborate and build their career in their respective professional expertise.
